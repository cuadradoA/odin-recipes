Classic Waffles

Descripsion

Classic waffles are a popular breakfast dish featuring a light, crispy exterior and a soft, fluffy interior. They are made by cooking a simple batter—composed of flour, eggs, milk, butter, baking powder, and sugar—in a specialized iron that creates their characteristic patterned surface

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt or to taste
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 ½ cups warm milk
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar together in a large bowl; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  3. Beat eggs in a separate bowl; stir in milk, butter, and vanilla.
  4. Pour milk mixture into flour mixture; beat until blended.
  5. Ladle batter into a preheated waffle iron.
  6. Cook waffles until golden and crisp.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
